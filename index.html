About Me

My goal is to bridge the gap between business and developers. With soft skills, a background in marketing, and the ability to code, I want to help reduce some of the friction that can happen when business people and developers collaborate.

I started out in marketing and got my BBA in it. I’ve worked at a marketing agency, an e-comm business, and done some freelancing too. While I really enjoyed the customer service, strategy, and design sides of marketing, I found myself wanting more. I love learning and pushing myself, and during a website redesign project, I found my next step: web development. Knowing how I work, I decided to dive in and learn web dev on my own—with the help of freeCodeCamp, The Odin Project, and lots of Googling, of course.
